# Stargazing Session Organised for Participants of FIP-2024
> Zenith Team on 27th September 2024

The annual conference of the School of Physics, University of Hyderabad - Frontiers In Physics 2024 was held on the 27th and 28th of September. 
All participants were invited for a stargazing session post-dinner on the night of the 27th. In an eventful session, participants witnessed 
the beauty of Jupiter in the clear night sky.

# Visit to BM Birla Science Centre
> Zenith Team on 22nd February 2025

On the 22nd of February 2025, the Zenith Astronomy Club conducted a visit to the BM Birla Science Centre and Planetarium for university students. The visit saw about 100 enthusiastic students of various disciplins
participating in the event. Participants explored a variety of interesting exhibits at the Museum, from ancient idols and sculptures to the incredible fossil collection and giant dinosaur replica.
The highlight of the visit was the Planetarium show, which provided an immersive experience of the universe, and topped off a day full of fun and exploration.
